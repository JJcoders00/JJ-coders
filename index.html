Welcome to Rubland

Rubland industries have been a recognized name in the kerala since 2005 and it is managed by Mr. Soy Vattapillil. We design and build robust smoke houses and allied products to help planters obtain higher-quality Ribbed Smoked Sheet (RSS).

Natural rubber in India is mainly produced by small holder farmers. Due to the lack of appropriate dryers, a significant portion of production is sold as low-grade Ribbed Smoked Sheet. Smoking sheets improves grade and value. Rubland's systems and services help increase the quality and price of rubber produced by planters.

Company Profile

Rubland is committed to quality installations, technical support and durable products. Our product line includes smoke houses, burners, chimneys, fans, fireboxes and complete process kits.

About JJ Coders

JJ Coders is a company started by Johan Jomet, built with a simple dream: to make kids and beginners fall in love with technology.
What started as a small initiative to teach basic coding has now grown into a creative platform where students learn Artificial Intelligence, coding, and digital innovation in a fun, practical, and playful way.
At JJ Coders, we believe that technology is not just about machines—it’s about imagination and creativity. That’s why we combine coding with AI, digital art, content creation, and real-world projects. From building websites and apps to experimenting with AI tools, every learner discovers how to create, share, and solve problems with technology.
We keep learning simple, accessible, and interactive. Whether you are writing your first line of code, making AI-generated designs, or creating YouTube Shorts with AI, JJ Coders makes the journey exciting and meaningful.
Our vision is to inspire a new generation of creators, innovators, and problem-solvers who are ready for the future.
Because at JJ Coders, we’re not just teaching coding or AI.
👉 We’re building creators of tomorrow.
